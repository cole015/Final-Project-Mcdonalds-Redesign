About McDonald's Philippines

Over 40 years of serving quality food and creating memorable experiences for Filipino families across the nation.

Our Story

McDonald's Philippines began its journey in 1981 when the first restaurant opened in Morayta, Manila. What started as a single location has grown into the country's leading quick-service restaurant chain.

From day one, our mission has been simple: to serve quality food quickly, in a clean environment, at a great value. We've adapted our global menu to Filipino tastes while maintaining the consistent quality that McDonald's is known for worldwide.

Today, we operate over 600 restaurants across the Philippines, employing thousands of Filipinos and serving millions of customers who trust us to deliver delicious food and exceptional service every day.

Our Journey

Key milestones in our Philippine story

1981

First Restaurant Opens

McDonald's Philippines opens its first restaurant in Morayta, Manila, marking the beginning of our Philippine journey.

1990

Drive-Thru Introduction

We introduced the first Drive-Thru service in the Philippines, revolutionizing fast food convenience.

2000

100th Store Milestone

Reached 100 restaurants nationwide, establishing McDonald's as a household name across the Philippines.

2010

Digital Innovation

Launched online ordering and delivery services, adapting to the digital age and changing customer preferences.

2020

500+ Restaurants

Expanded to over 500 locations nationwide, continuing to serve Filipino families in more communities.

2024

Sustainable Future

Committed to sustainability initiatives and eco-friendly practices while maintaining our promise of quality food.
